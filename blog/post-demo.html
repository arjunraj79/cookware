Introducing Our New Pressure Cooker — Fast, Safe, Reliable

Dec 16, 2025 • ONS Cookers

We're excited to launch our newest pressure cooker designed for everyday kitchens. Built with tri-ply stainless steel, it ensures even heat distribution, improved durability, and effortless cleaning.

Key Features

  • Tri-ply stainless steel body for even heating
  • Multiple safety features including pressure lock and quick-release valve
  • Ergonomic handles and easy-to-read pressure indicators
  • Quick-release valve and dishwasher-safe parts

How to Use

Wash before first use. Add ingredients and liquid according to recipe, lock lid, bring to pressure, then reduce heat and cook for recommended time. Use the quick-release valve for immediate pressure release or allow natural release for slow-finish dishes.

This cooker shortens common recipes from hours to minutes without sacrificing flavor. It's perfect for busy weeknights and makes traditional dishes quick and convenient. Pair with our cookware sets for complete kitchen solutions.
