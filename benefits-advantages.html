Advantages of ONS

Safe

  • Pressure-locked Safety Lid: The lid is automatically locked under pressure, similar to an airplane door. It cannot open until pressure drops to a safe level.
  • Automatic Safety Valve: When activated, steam and food are safely vented downward.

Better Pressure Regulation

The new improved pressure regulator controls pressure better, cooks faster, saves fuel, and is easy to install and remove. It also reduces dal frothing. (Available in all ONS variants except Stainless Steel and Big PEXA.)

Trouble-Free

  • Longer-lasting Sealing Ring/Gasket: The inside-fitting lid reduces wear and protects the gasket from food acids.
  • Rust-proof Components: A special alloy handle bar and rust-proof brackets avoid corrosion.
