Product Certification

UNDERWRITERS LABORATORIES INC (UL)

Pressure cookers with the UL Mark and listed by Underwriters Laboratories Inc. (UL) are manufactured by ONS Cookers. UL is a global safety analysis and certification body founded in 1894, headquartered in Chicago. Its mark signifies rigid safety compliance and ongoing inspections.
